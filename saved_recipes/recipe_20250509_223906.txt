Recipe:
1. Recipe Name:  Masaledar Paneer & Mushroom Masala (Extra Spicy North Indian)

2. Ingredients Needed:

* 250 gms Paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cubed
* 250 gms Mushrooms, sliced (Button or mixed variety)
* 1 large Onion, finely chopped
* 2-3 Green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to your spice preference, add more for extra extra spicy!)
* 1 inch Ginger, grated
* 2 cloves Garlic, minced
* 1 tsp Red Chili Powder (Kashmiri for color, or Byadgi for extra heat)
* 1 tsp Coriander Powder
* 1 tsp Cumin Powder
* ½ tsp Turmeric Powder
* ½ tsp Garam Masala
* ¼ tsp Asafoetida (hing)
* 2-3 tbsp Oil (vegetable or mustard)
* 1-2 Tomatoes, pureed
* ½ cup chopped Cilantro (coriander leaves)
* Salt to taste
* 1 Green chili, slit (for garnish)


3. Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan or kadai. Add asafoetida and let it splutter.
2. Add the chopped onions and saute until golden brown.
3. Add the green chilies, ginger, and garlic. Saute for a minute until fragrant.
4. Add the red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and turmeric powder. Saute for 30 seconds, stirring continuously to prevent burning.
5. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the masala (around 5-7 minutes).
6. Add the sliced mushrooms and saute for 5 minutes until slightly softened.
7. Add the paneer cubes and saute gently for 2-3 minutes. Be careful not to break the paneer.
8. Add salt to taste and mix well.
9. Sprinkle garam masala and cilantro. Mix gently.
10. Garnish with a slit green chili.
11. Serve hot with roti, naan, or brown rice.

4. Estimated Calories: Approximately 450-500 calories per serving (depending on portion size and oil used).

5. Cuisine Type: North Indian

6. Dietary Info: Vegetarian, can be made vegan by substituting paneer with tofu or other plant-based protein.  Contains gluten if served with wheat-based bread.

7. Recommended Meals for the Day:

* **Breakfast:**  Oatmeal with fruits and nuts, or a light vegetable and paneer paratha.
* **Lunch:**  A lighter salad with mixed vegetables and a small portion of brown rice.
* **Dinner:**  This Masaledar Paneer & Mushroom Masala.
* **Snacks:**  A small bowl of fresh fruits or a handful of nuts.


**Note:** Adjust the amount of green chilies according to your spice tolerance.  For an even spicier dish, you can add a pinch of black pepper or a few drops of your favourite chili oil at the end.  Enjoy!
