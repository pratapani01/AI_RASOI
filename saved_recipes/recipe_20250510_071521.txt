Recipe:
1. Recipe Name:  Masala Mushroom & Onion Sabzi with Brown Rice

2. Ingredients Needed:

* 250 gms Mushrooms (Button or Oyster), cleaned and sliced
* 1 large Onion, finely chopped
* 2 cloves Garlic, minced
* 1 inch Ginger, minced
* 1 Green Chili, finely chopped (adjust to your spice preference)
* 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
* 1 tsp Coriander Powder
* ½ tsp Red Chili Powder (adjust to your spice preference)
* ½ tsp Garam Masala
* ¼ tsp Asafoetida (hing)
* 2 tbsp Oil (Olive or Canola)
* Salt to taste
* Fresh Coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
* 1 cup Brown Rice (cooked)


3. Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium heat. Add asafoetida and let it splutter for a few seconds.
2. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.  
3. Add ginger and garlic and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
4. Add green chilies and sauté briefly.
5. Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, and red chili powder. Sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant, stirring constantly to prevent burning.
6. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté for 5-7 minutes until they soften and release their moisture.
7. Stir in salt and garam masala. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
9. Serve hot with cooked brown rice.


4. Estimated Calories:  Approximately 350-400 calories (per serving, depending on portion size and type of oil used).
