Recipe:
1. Recipe Name:  Saffron-Infused Chicken & Paneer Tikka Masala with Brown Rice

2. Ingredients Needed:

* **For the Tikka:**
    * 250g boneless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes
    * 150g paneer, cut into 1-inch cubes
    * 1 tbsp Greek yogurt
    * 1 tbsp lemon juice
    * ½ tsp turmeric powder
    * ½ tsp garam masala
    * ¼ tsp red chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
    * ¼ tsp cumin powder
    * ¼ tsp coriander powder
    * A pinch of saffron strands, soaked in 1 tbsp warm milk
    * Salt to taste
* **For the Masala:**
    * 1 tbsp olive oil
    * 1 medium onion, finely chopped
    * 1 inch ginger, grated
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 1 small tomato, pureed
    * ½ tsp red chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
    * ½ tsp garam masala
    * ¼ tsp turmeric powder
    * ¼ tsp cumin powder
    * ¼ tsp coriander powder
    * 1 cup vegetable broth or water
    * Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
* **Sides:**
    * 1 cup brown rice, cooked


3. Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Marinate the Tikka:** In a bowl, combine chicken, paneer, yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric powder, garam masala, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, saffron milk, and salt. Mix well and marinate for at least 30 minutes (longer is better).
2. **Prepare the Masala:** Heat olive oil in a pan. Add onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger and garlic and sauté for another minute.
3. **Add the puree:** Stir in tomato puree and cook for 2-3 minutes until the oil starts to separate.
4. **Spice it up:** Add red chili powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, cumin powder, and coriander powder. Sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
5. **Simmer:** Pour in vegetable broth, bring to a simmer, and cook for 5 minutes.
6. **Add the Tikka:** Add the marinated chicken and paneer to the pan. Stir gently and cook until the chicken is cooked through and the paneer is heated (about 10-12 minutes).
7. **Garnish & Serve:** Garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve hot with cooked brown rice.
