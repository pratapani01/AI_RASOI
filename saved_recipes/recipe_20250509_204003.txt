Recipe:
1. Recipe Name:  Saffron Chicken with Brown Rice & Mixed Vegetables

2. Ingredients Needed:

* 250 gms boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes
* 1 cup brown rice
* 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, cauliflower – frozen or fresh)
* 1 medium onion, finely chopped
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 inch ginger, minced
* 1 green chili, finely chopped (adjust to your spice preference)
* 1 tsp turmeric powder
* 1 tsp coriander powder
* ½ tsp cumin powder
* ½ tsp garam masala
* ¼ tsp saffron strands, soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk
* 2 tbsp olive oil
* Salt to taste
* Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish
* 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth (or water)


3. Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Rice:** Rinse the brown rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Cook the rice according to package directions, usually with a 2:1 water to rice ratio.
2. **Sauté Aromatics:** Heat olive oil in a pan or pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add the minced garlic, ginger, and green chili; sauté for another minute until fragrant.
3. **Cook the Chicken:** Add the chicken cubes to the pan and cook until browned on all sides.
4. **Add Spices:** Stir in the turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and garam masala. Cook for a minute, stirring continuously, until fragrant.
5. **Simmer:** Add the mixed vegetables and chicken broth (or water). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
6. **Incorporate Saffron:** Stir in the saffron milk mixture.
7. **Serve:**  Serve the chicken and vegetable mixture over the cooked brown rice. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
